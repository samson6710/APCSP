About Webb Biology/Health

At the Webb School of Biology/Health, we are committed to promoting biology and health sciences in ways that are within the reach of our students. We want to produce research that contributes to society and improves human health through innovation and discovery.

Our Mission

We believe that biology has the power to transform societies and the lives of individuals. Our research is extensive: PLGA polymers, neuroscience, and the growth cycle of Drosophila. They can each help restore soft tissue in humans, help restore hearing, and help scientists with assortment experiments!

Our Team

Our team includes all of the members of Webb Global Health as well as the biology enthusiasts who participate in biology team competitions and research with me.

Our Achievements

We are proud of our contribution to biology: we have been honored as semi finalists in the Conrad International Competition, and we are preparing our research paper for publication in a journal.
